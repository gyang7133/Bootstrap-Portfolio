About Me

First generation Hmong-American woman living in Atlanta with a passion for technology. Graduated from Georgie Gwinnet College (GGC) with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology focused in Systems and Security.

I currently work as a Lifecycle Operations engineer at GE Digital. I’m proud to work for an organization that appreciates the competitive advantage of a diverse workforce, as well as its social impact

Outside my career, I’m a part-time student enrolled at the Georgia Tech Coding Boot Camp. I also volunteer as one of the program manager liaisons for the Women in Technology Campus Atlanta organization to recruit, mentor, and retain college women in STEAM careers. I play volleyball and enjoy traveling, cooking, and participating at local tech conferences and workshops in my local community.
